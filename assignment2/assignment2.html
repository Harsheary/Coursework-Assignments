Websites about Protected Areas Issues

Protected areas, encompassing national parks, wildlife reserves, and marine sanctuaries, are vital for conserving biodiversity and ecosystems. These areas serve as havens for endangered species, safeguarding their habitats from human encroachment and exploitation. Moreover, protected areas play a crucial role in mitigating climate change by sequestering carbon and maintaining ecological balance. As global biodiversity faces unprecedented threats, the preservation of these areas is paramount to ensuring the long-term sustainability of our planet's natural resources and ecosystems.
